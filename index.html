About CoreStorm Global

Founded in Sydney to make good energy easy to source

CoreStorm Global began in 2018 with a simple frustration: sourcing genuinely good renewable hardware was harder than it should be. So we built direct relationships with Tier-1 panel manufacturers, leading inverter makers, and battery & BMS engineers — and cut out the noise in between.

We inspect and bench-test everything, hold real stock in our Alexandria warehouse, and run our own export pipeline to the UK and Europe. That means honest specs, fixed DDP pricing, and one team accountable from quote to commissioning.

  • Tier-1 only. Every product is standards-tested and warranty-backed — no grey-market shortcuts.
  • Honestly priced. Direct sourcing means no importer-distributor-retailer triple margin.
  • There after the sale. A 25-year performance warranty, monitored and serviced locally in every market.
